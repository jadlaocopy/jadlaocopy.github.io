About Me

With a 4 year background in nanotech, I've combined my technical know-how, my interest in innovative technology, and my writing skills.


I have worked hands-on in the lab with things such as synthesising nanomaterials, organic and inorganic chemicals, analytical balances, electrophoretic techniques, spectroscopy, various forms of microscopy, general lab equipment, software, and more.


In the past, I have worked with a government agency involved in anti-money laundering, and the Australian Red Cross Blood Service's recent implementation of an online database system for doctors.


Now, I want to take my own varied perspective on tech, and help companies that have an eye for innovation and building a better world.
