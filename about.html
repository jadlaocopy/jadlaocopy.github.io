About Me

Josef has combined his technical know-how, with his love for learning and writing. He is uniquely fitted to write technology copy with a degree in Nanotechnology from the University of New South Wales in Sydney.


Throughout his education, he enjoyed working hands-on in the laboratory, and has experience with things such as synthesising nanomaterials, organic and inorganic chemicals, analytical balances, autoclaves, electrophoretic techniques, spectroscopy, various forms of microscopy, general lab equipment, and more. Now, he helps software and technology companies to generate leads and sales.


He writes website content, product descriptions, brochures, white papers, and more. All with the aim of educating your prospects, moving them along your sales funnel and impacting your bottom line.
